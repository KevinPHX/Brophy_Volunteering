Instructions

This site is specifically for Brophy students.

You will need to create an account and login. This will help the tutor know how to get ahold of you.

Once you have an account you can either "Request A Tutor" or choose an Tutor from available appointments in the Calendar.

When you click on the appointed time in the Calendar it will tell you what subjects that tutor is comfortable tutoring.

Any questions, please contact Mrs. Venberg at kvenberg@brophyprep.org
